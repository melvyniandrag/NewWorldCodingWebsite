Mission Statement

These things aren't useful without expert guidance. A 4090 RTX with 24GB RAM? An M4 Max Macbook Pro? A case full of Raspberry Pis? It's easier to buy these things than it is to use them to their full potential.

At New World Coding, we not only offer the latest and greatest hardware, but we also provide amazing classes where you can learn how to harness its full power. Whether you're interested in humble, energy-efficient microcontrollers or running massively parallel programs at blazing speeds, we can teach you how to do it. Join us and discover just how incredible technology can be when you know how to do things right.
