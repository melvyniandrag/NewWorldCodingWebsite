How to Be Good With Computers

The way to get good at programming, video editing, computer graphics - or any technical thing for that matter - is to practice consistently. And there is no better time or place in history to get good with technology than here and now. Youtube, Udemy, Coursera or the public library are all great places to learn for free or at negligible cost. You can get powerful used computers shipped to your door for about $100, or invest a little bit more and get a state-of-the-art machine from a popular manufacturer. The power to succeed is within you, and with all these tools and resources at your fingertips, you're sure to achieve your goals.

And why not take it a step further while you're studying? At New World Coding we offer fun, inspiring classes to supplement your education. In our workshops we'll show you many interesting things you haven't seen before, reinforce things you've already learned, and tie it all together through a series of hands-on, engaging projects. Have a look at the list of offerings below and consider signing up for one, two or all of them! You'll gain new skills, have a great time, and walk away with some completed projects you can show your friends, use in your homelab, or put on your resume.
